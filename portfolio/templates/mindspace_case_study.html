MindSpace – Your Mental Health Companion

Project Overview

Mental health support should be simple, safe, and accessible to everyone. This project focused on designing a mobile application that provides users with tools to manage their emotional well-being, track their moods, and develop mindfulness routines.

MindSpace is a fictional mental wellness app built around gentle UX, soft visual aesthetics, and a feature-rich but simple flow. The goal was to create a mobile-first experience that lowers barriers to mental health care and builds trust through thoughtful design and empathetic content.

Timeframe

3 Weeks

Objectives

Wireframes

Lo-fi wireframes defined the core flows for mood tracking, journaling, and guided meditation. In hi-fi, we introduced soft gradients, calm typography, and friendly illustrations to build a gentle, supportive environment for mental well-being.

Lo-Fi Wireframe of Mindspace

Branding & Visual Design

  • Colors: Pastel tones (lavender, teal, soft peach)
  • Typography: Rounded sans-serif for warmth
  • Logo: Minimal brain/heart + leaf symbol
  • Imagery: Organic shapes and line illustrations

High-Fidelity Screens

  1. Onboarding: Soft animations and 3-step flow
  2. Mood Tracker: Emoji icons and tags
  3. Journal: Gratitude + prompts
  4. Calendar: Mood heatmap
  5. Affirmations: Calming quotes + animations
  6. Insights: Graphs, trends, suggestions

Usability Testing

To validate design decisions, remote usability testing was conducted with 5 users via Maze.

Test Scenario

  1. Open the app and complete onboarding
  2. Log a mood using emoji input
  3. Write a short journal entry
  4. View emotional insights in the stats dashboard

Key Findings

  • Task Completion: 100% of users successfully completed the flow
  • Onboarding: Described as “calming” and “gentle,” but some preferred fewer steps
  • Affirmations: Users requested faster access to affirmations
  • Navigation: Overall intuitive, mood tracker highlighted as favorite feature
“It feels like a quiet space to think.”
“I love how simple and soothing everything is.”

Iterations Based on Feedback

  • Reduced onboarding steps from 3 to 2 for quicker entry
  • Added a journaling streak progress ring to boost motivation
  • Introduced mood logging shortcuts from the home screen for efficiency
