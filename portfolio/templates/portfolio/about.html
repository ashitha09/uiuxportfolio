Hi! I'm Ashitha, a UI/UX designer and web developer with a background in Computer Science and a deep passion for creating meaningful, intuitive digital experiences.

My design journey began through coding — but I quickly discovered that what excited me most was not just how things work, but how people feel when they use them. Today, I combine logical problem-solving with creative thinking to craft user interfaces that are not only beautiful but purposeful.

I specialize in:

  • Designing minimal and modern web experiences
  • Building interactive prototypes in Figma
  • Developing responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Django
  • Applying user-centered design principles backed by research and empathy

Whether it's designing a brand-new product or refining an existing one, I focus on clarity, usability, and visual storytelling. I'm also continuously learning — currently diving deeper into accessibility and motion design.

Let’s create something thoughtful and impactful together.
