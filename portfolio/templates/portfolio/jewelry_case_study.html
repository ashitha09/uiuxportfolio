Designing an Online Experience for a Modern Jewelry Brand

Project Overview

Jewelry is deeply personal—each piece tells a story. This project aimed to translate that feeling into a seamless digital experience. The brand is built around the values of elegance, simplicity, and sustainability.

This fictional brand is rooted in nature, using recycled metals and conflict-free stones. The design merges luxury with ease of use to reach a modern, conscious audience shopping online for meaningful pieces.

Timeframe

4 Weeks

My Role

UX Research, UI Design, Prototyping, Branding, Visual Design, Usability Testing

Tools

Figma, Balsamic, Google Forms, Zoom

The Challenge

  • Create a homepage that immediately communicates brand values
  • Design a discovery experience with product filters
  • Streamline navigation and shopping flow
  • Incorporate blog and storytelling elements for user engagement

Objectives

  • Create an elegant, responsive website for the jewelry brand
  • Allow users to easily discover, filter, and shop products
  • Highlight craftsmanship, material sourcing, and brand story
  • Build trust and curiosity through thoughtful content and layout
  • Design intuitive interactions and visual hierarchy

Research & Strategy

User Persona Highlights

  • Primary Users: Women aged 20–45 looking for elegant, ethical, and everyday jewelry
  • Needs: Simplicity, clarity, mobile-friendly shopping, trust in product quality and sourcing
  • Pain Points: Overly cluttered interfaces, lack of information about materials, poor mobile UX

Persona 1: Young professional, values elegance & convenience

Persona 2: Conscious buyer, values sustainability & quality

Usability Testing

Scenario

Conducted remote usability testing with 4 participants (women aged 22–40). Participants were asked to complete the following tasks:

  • Find a gold necklace under £150 using the product filters
  • Explore the "Discover" page and explain what the brand stands for
  • Add a product to the cart and proceed to checkout
  • Locate the blog/storytelling section

Outcome

  • Task Completion: All participants completed the tasks successfully.
  • Filtering System: 3/4 participants found it intuitive and quick to use.
  • Discover Page: 100% appreciated storytelling & sustainability focus.
  • Navigation: 1 participant initially struggled to find the Cart section.

Metrics: Task success rate 95%, average task time 1.5 minutes, minimal errors.
